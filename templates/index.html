Income Prediction - Adult Census Data.

The Goal is to predict whether a person has an income of more than 50K a year or not. This is basically a binary classification problem where a person is classified into the more than 50K group or less than 50K group. The project is implemented with continious integration and continious deployment. The models used for the training can be updated and the Income can be predicted with a user input.
